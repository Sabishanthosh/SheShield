Instagram Blackmail

Someone threatening to share your private photos/videos unless you pay or comply with demands.

⚠️

This is a criminal offense under IT Act Section 66E and IPC 354C. You are the victim — not at fault.

Warning Signs

  • 🔴 Stranger sent follow request, started friendly conversation
  • 🔴 Asked for personal photos "just between us"
  • 🔴 Now threatening to share photos with family/friends
  • 🔴 Demanding money via UPI, crypto, or gift cards

What To Do — Step by Step

  1. 1Do NOT pay. Payment encourages more blackmail.
  2. 2Screenshot everything — threats, messages, profile.
  3. 3Block the person on all platforms.
  4. 4Report to Instagram — Profile → Report → It's inappropriate.
  5. 5File cyber complaint at cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
  6. 6Tell a trusted adult — you are not alone.

WhatsApp Scam

Fraudsters impersonate friends, family, or officials to steal money or personal data via WhatsApp.

⚠️

Never share OTPs, bank details, or Aadhaar on WhatsApp — no real official will ask for these.

Common Scam Types

  • 🔴 "Mom/Sis, I'm in trouble — send money urgently" (impersonation)
  • 🔴 Fake job offer with upfront fee payment
  • 🔴 Lottery winner — "Click this link to claim prize"
  • 🔴 WhatsApp OTP theft — "Forward this code, it came by mistake"
  • 🔴 Video call recording and blackmail

What To Do

  1. 1Verify by calling back on a known number.
  2. 2Never click unknown links received on WhatsApp.
  3. 3Screenshot the conversation and save to Evidence Vault.
  4. 4Block and Report — Chat → More → Report.
  5. 5Report fraud at cybercrime.gov.in or 1930.

Fake Profile Reporting

Someone created a fake account using your name, photos, or identity to harass or defame you.

⚠️

Under IT Act Section 66C, identity theft is punishable with up to 3 years imprisonment and fine.

How to Identify a Fake Profile

  • 🔴 Account uses your photos without permission
  • 🔴 Messaging your friends/family pretending to be you
  • 🔴 Posting objectionable content in your name
  • 🔴 Recently created account with few followers

Report by Platform

📸 Instagram

Profile → ⋮ → Report → Pretending to be someone

👥 Facebook

Profile → ⋯ → Find Support → Pretending to be me

🐦 Twitter/X

Profile → ⋯ → Report → Impersonation

💬 WhatsApp

Chat → More → Report contact to WhatsApp

Quick Safety Tips

🔐

Strong Passwords

Use 12+ characters with symbols. Never reuse passwords across accounts.

🔒

2-Step Verification

Enable 2FA on Instagram, WhatsApp, Gmail. Blocks access even if password is stolen.

👁️

Privacy Settings

Set Instagram/Facebook to Private. Only accept requests from known people.

🚫

Don't Share OTPs

No bank or government will ever ask for your OTP. Sharing it gives full account access.

📸

Photo Safety

Never share intimate photos digitally. Once sent, you lose control forever.

🔗

Suspicious Links

Don't click links from unknown numbers or DMs — even if they look official.
